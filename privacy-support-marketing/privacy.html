English

Data storage: All your behavior logs and settings are stored only on your device (and within any sync you enable, e.g. iCloud). We do not upload your personal data to our own servers.

Data use: We do not sell or share your data with third parties. All statistics and displays in the app are based on data on your device.

Subscriptions & payment: Subscriptions and payments are handled by Apple. We only receive anonymous subscription status information to unlock features.

Updates: We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.

中文

数据存储：您的所有行为记录与设置仅保存在您的设备上（及您启用的 iCloud 等同步范围内），我们不会将您的个人数据上传到自己的服务器。

数据使用：我们不会出售或与第三方共享您的数据。应用内的统计与展示均基于您设备上的本地数据。

订阅与支付：订阅与支付由 Apple 处理，我们仅接收与订阅状态相关的匿名信息，用于解锁功能。

更新：我们可能不定期更新本政策。继续使用应用即表示接受更新后的政策。
