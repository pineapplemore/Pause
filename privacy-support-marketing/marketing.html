English

Pause helps you notice and track body-focused repetitive behaviors (BFRB) and similar habits—such as skin picking, hair pulling, lip or nail biting—in a simple, private way. You choose up to four behaviors to track; each tap records one instance. View your patterns in the calendar and statistics, and export reports if you subscribe.

This app is for personal awareness and tracking only. It is not a substitute for professional care. If you need clinical support, please seek a qualified provider.

中文

本应用仅供个人觉察与记录，不能替代专业诊疗。如需临床支持，请寻求合格的专业人士。
