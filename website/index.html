Marketing / 营销说明

中文

噗噗日记（PuffDiary）面向肠易激（IBS）等有记录需求的用户，也适合单纯好奇、想了解自己习惯的朋友。记录每日排气次数与类型，通过日历与统计查看规律，并可导出 PDF 报告。本应用不用于诊断或治疗任何疾病，仅供个人记录与参考。

English

PuffDiary is for anyone who wants to track passing gas—whether you have IBS, digestive sensitivity, or are simply curious about your habits. Log daily counts and types, view calendar and statistics, and export PDF reports. This app is not for diagnosing or treating any condition; it’s for personal tracking and curiosity only.
